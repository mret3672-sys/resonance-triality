### Operator S — Squeeze (Compress)

**Reduce intensity while preserving trace and topology. False structure collapses.**

### Operator I — Invert (Flip Perspective)

**Swap inside↔outside, dominant↔subordinate across the center seam.**

### Operator R — Recur (Chamber Re-entry)

**When pressure exceeds local holding capacity, identity recurs rather than disappears.**
